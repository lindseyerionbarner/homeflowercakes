    About us

    Welcome to Homeflower Cakes! I'm Lindsey - scientist by day and baker by night - I love all things beautiful and all things cake. I dreamed of operating an independent cake kitchen ever since my sister was crazy enough to trust me with making her wedding cake before I had any experience. At Homeflower Cakes, we provide handmade cakes in the city of Boston, and our priority is to make delicious products that do not trade off flavor for style. We operate in the heart of Fenway and thrive in the vibrant Boston community; our goal is to help connect other rising small businesses and entrepreneurs to strengthen our commmunity-driven ecosystem.

    Allergen information

    We take food allergies seriously; please let us know if you have an allergy so that we can take all precautions to prevent cross-contact with allergens. All of our products can be made fully gluten free and celiac safe. We do not yet offer vegan or dairy-free options.
